Why Camp?

“These are the feasts of the LORD, holy convocations which you shall proclaim at their appointed times.”
Leviticus 23:4

As God was creating the nation of Isreal He made a point to include yearly feasts, special times set apart to meet with Him. We believe that it’s still God’s heart that His people set apart special times to be with Him. That’s why we make camp a part of our year!

Join us for a weekend of powerful Bible teaching, passionate worship, and meaningful fellowship. Join us for a weekend set apart to God!
