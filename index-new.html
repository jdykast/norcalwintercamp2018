NorCal Winter Camp

January 27-29, 2018

Strong.

In Grace.

“You therefore, my son, be strong in the grace that is in Christ Jesus.”
2 Timothy 2:1

Paul fills his letters with exhortations like this one. Be strong in grace! And yet, there seems to be a paradox here. Grace isn’t about our strength. Grace isn’t about our efforts. Grace is God’s work.

This year we want to focus on God’s amazing grace and the radical transformations that it brings. Grace truly changes everything!

Join us for a weekend of powerful Bible teaching, passionate worship, and meaningful fellowship. Join us for a weekend set apart to God!
